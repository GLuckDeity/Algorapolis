## 2. Detailed Principle Descriptions

### Cyber Domain Principles

#### **[CYB-7] Supply Chain Integrity Mandate**
- **Original ID:** DP-7
- **Priority:** P1
- **Source Section:** §1 in [01-cyber-civilization-resilience-deeper-research.md](file:///d:/GitHub/Algorapolis/research/studies/deeper-research/01-cyber-civilization-resilience-deeper-research.md)
- **Integration Target:** Core §15 (Security Architecture) — SLE Supply Chain Integrity Module

All software deployed in governance infrastructure shall have verified build provenance (SLSA Level 3+), complete dependency mapping, and continuous vulnerability monitoring. The SLE shall fund critical open-source dependencies proportional to their centrality in the dependency graph.

#### **[CYB-8] Calibrated Trust Architecture**
- **Original ID:** DP-8
- **Priority:** P2
- **Source Section:** §2 in [01-cyber-civilization-resilience-deeper-research.md](file:///d:/GitHub/Algorapolis/research/studies/deeper-research/01-cyber-civilization-resilience-deeper-research.md)
- **Integration Target:** Core §15 (Security Architecture) — SLE Trust Tier Classification

Zero-trust shall be implemented as a four-tier calibrated architecture (Critical Governance, Operational Governance, Citizen Services, Public Information), not as a uniform policy. Verification overhead shall not exceed defined latency thresholds for each tier.

#### **[CYB-9] Privacy-Preserving Verification**
- **Original ID:** DP-9
- **Priority:** P2
- **Source Section:** §2 in [01-cyber-civilization-resilience-deeper-research.md](file:///d:/GitHub/Algorapolis/research/studies/deeper-research/01-cyber-civilization-resilience-deeper-research.md)
- **Integration Target:** Core §15 (Security Architecture) + Core §5 (Education — Data Sovereignty bridge)

All citizen-facing identity verification shall use privacy-enhancing technologies (zero-knowledge proofs, selective disclosure, homomorphic encryption) that allow citizens to prove eligibility without revealing unnecessary personal data.

#### **[CYB-10] AI Cyber Defense with Human Override**
- **Original ID:** DP-10
- **Priority:** P2
- **Source Section:** §3 in [01-cyber-civilization-resilience-deeper-research.md](file:///d:/GitHub/Algorapolis/research/studies/deeper-research/01-cyber-civilization-resilience-deeper-research.md)
- **Integration Target:** Core §14 (Intelligence Architecture) — SLE AI Defense Protocol

AI cyber defense systems shall implement confidence-weighted action, mandatory patch validation, and escalation circuit breakers that inject human oversight when AI-vs-AI interactions exceed defined thresholds.

#### **[CYB-11] Federated Identity Architecture**
- **Original ID:** DP-11
- **Priority:** P1
- **Source Section:** §4 in [01-cyber-civilization-resilience-deeper-research.md](file:///d:/GitHub/Algorapolis/research/studies/deeper-research/01-cyber-civilization-resilience-deeper-research.md)
- **Integration Target:** Core §15 (Security Architecture) — SLE Identity Module

The SLE shall never maintain a centralized identity database. Identity data shall be stored in distributed, encrypted form with the citizen as primary custodian. All identity credentials shall be designed for rapid revocation and re-issuance.

#### **[CYB-12] Physical Layer Resilience**
- **Original ID:** DP-12
- **Priority:** P2
- **Source Section:** §5 in [01-cyber-civilization-resilience-deeper-research.md](file:///d:/GitHub/Algorapolis/research/studies/deeper-research/01-cyber-civilization-resilience-deeper-research.md)
- **Integration Target:** Core §15 (Security Architecture) — NDT Infrastructure Layer

No jurisdiction shall depend on fewer than three geographically diverse submarine cable connections. Satellite backup capacity for essential governance functions shall be pre-deployed and tested quarterly. No single private entity shall control more than 30% of civilizational internet backbone capacity.

#### **[CYB-13] Quantum-Resilient Cryptography**
- **Original ID:** DP-13
- **Priority:** P0
- **Source Section:** §6 in [01-cyber-civilization-resilience-deeper-research.md](file:///d:/GitHub/Algorapolis/research/studies/deeper-research/01-cyber-civilization-resilience-deeper-research.md)
- **Integration Target:** Core §15 (Security Architecture) — SLE Cryptographic Module

All cryptographic implementations shall be crypto-agile. Hybrid cryptography (classical + post-quantum) shall be mandatory for all new deployments immediately. Data with confidentiality requirements exceeding 10 years shall be encrypted with post-quantum algorithms regardless of current threat level.

#### **[CYB-14] Q-Day Preparedness**
- **Original ID:** DP-14
- **Priority:** P0
- **Source Section:** §6 in [01-cyber-civilization-resilience-deeper-research.md](file:///d:/GitHub/Algorapolis/research/studies/deeper-research/01-cyber-civilization-resilience-deeper-research.md)
- **Integration Target:** Core §15 (Security Architecture) — SLE Crisis Protocol

The SLE shall maintain a Q-Day Preparedness Protocol with pre-staged migration packages enabling complete cryptographic transition within 72 hours of a Q-Day declaration. The protocol shall be tested annually.

#### **[CYB-15] Cyber Resilience Equalization**
- **Original ID:** DP-15
- **Priority:** P2
- **Source Section:** §7 in [01-cyber-civilization-resilience-deeper-research.md](file:///d:/GitHub/Algorapolis/research/studies/deeper-research/01-cyber-civilization-resilience-deeper-research.md)
- **Integration Target:** Core §15 (Security Architecture) + Core §22 (Ecology — equity bridge)

The SLE shall establish a minimum cyber resilience floor for all jurisdictions, financed through a Cyber Resilience Equalization Fund. No jurisdiction shall be left below the cyber poverty line. Cyber attacks on any jurisdiction shall trigger collective defense protocols.

#### **[CYB-16] CCRI Constitutional Metric**
- **Original ID:** DP-16
- **Priority:** P3
- **Source Section:** §8 in [01-cyber-civilization-resilience-deeper-research.md](file:///d:/GitHub/Algorapolis/research/studies/deeper-research/01-cyber-civilization-resilience-deeper-research.md)
- **Integration Target:** Core §15 (Security Architecture) — NDT Risk Layer

The Civilizational Cyber Resilience Index (CCRI) shall be maintained as a constitutional governance metric with eight dimensions. No dimension shall fall below 60/100 for more than 90 consecutive days without triggering constitutional review of cyber resilience investment.

#### **[CYB-17] Attribution and Accountability**
- **Original ID:** DP-17
- **Priority:** P3
- **Source Section:** §8 in [01-cyber-civilization-resilience-deeper-research.md](file:///d:/GitHub/Algorapolis/research/studies/deeper-research/01-cyber-civilization-resilience-deeper-research.md)
- **Integration Target:** Core §14 (Intelligence Architecture) — SLE Attribution Module

The SLE shall invest in and maintain cyber attribution capabilities sufficient to identify the source of significant attacks with defined confidence levels, enabling deterrence through accountability. Attribution findings shall be published when confidence exceeds defined thresholds, even when politically inconvenient.

### Ecology Domain Principles

#### **[ECO-6] Planetary Boundary Hard Constraint Protocol**
- **Original ID:** DP-6
- **Priority:** P0
- **Source Section:** §1 in [02-ecological-optimization-deeper-research.md](file:///d:/GitHub/Algorapolis/research/studies/deeper-research/02-ecological-optimization-deeper-research.md)
- **Integration Target:** Core §22 (Ecological Stewardship) — SLE Constraint Engine

All nine planetary boundaries shall be encoded as hard constraints in the SLE. No economic activity shall be permitted that would push a control variable from SAFE to ZONE, or from ZONE to HAZARD. Boundary transgression shall trigger automatic governance responses calibrated to the severity of transgression, as defined by the SAFE/ZONE/HAZARD protocol. Override of HAZARD protocols shall require an 80% supermajority of the Citizens' Assembly with a 90-day maximum duration and verified existential justification.

#### **[ECO-7] Coupled Boundary Assessment**
- **Original ID:** DP-7
- **Priority:** P0
- **Source Section:** §1 in [02-ecological-optimization-deeper-research.md](file:///d:/GitHub/Algorapolis/research/studies/deeper-research/02-ecological-optimization-deeper-research.md)
- **Integration Target:** Core §22 (Ecological Stewardship) — NDT Coupled Model

The NDT shall model planetary boundary interactions as a coupled system. A HAZARD declaration on any boundary shall trigger mandatory ZONE assessment of all coupled boundaries within 24 hours. The SLE shall optimize across the full boundary interaction matrix, not per-boundary in isolation. Coupling coefficients shall be derived from the Lade et al. (2020) interaction model and updated as Earth system science advances.

#### **[ECO-8] Precautionary Default for Novel Entities**
- **Original ID:** DP-8
- **Priority:** P2
- **Source Section:** §1 in [02-ecological-optimization-deeper-research.md](file:///d:/GitHub/Algorapolis/research/studies/deeper-research/02-ecological-optimization-deeper-research.md)
- **Integration Target:** Core §22 (Ecological Stewardship) — SLE Novel Entity Gate

Any substance not previously evaluated against planetary boundary criteria shall be classified as "potentially boundary-threatening" by default. Production, import, or deployment of such substances shall require a Novel Entity Impact Assessment (NEIA) demonstrating that the substance does not contribute to boundary transgression, prior to authorization. The burden of proof shall rest on the entity proposing deployment, not on the governance system.

#### **[EDU-5] Inverse-Need Funding Formula**
- **Original ID:** Principle 5
- **Priority:** P1
- **Source Section:** §2 in [04-education-equality-deeper-research.md](file:///d:/GitHub/Algorapolis/research/studies/deeper-research/04-education-equality-deeper-research.md)
- **Integration Target:** Core §5 (Education) — SLE Resource Allocation Engine

The SLE shall allocate educational resources through an inverse-need formula: per-student investment shall increase as a function of cumulative disadvantage indicators, not decrease. The funding function is f(x) = B + Σ(wᵢ × dᵢ), where B is a base allocation equal for all students, wᵢ are calibrated weights, and dᵢ are disadvantage dimensions (socioeconomic status, disability status, language minority status, geographic remoteness, historical exclusion). The function is monotonic: more need always produces more funding, never less.
